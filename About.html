About

I am Joseph Crispell - an epidemiologist working at UCD. My research focuses on the transmission dynamics of bovine tuberculosis - a respiratory disease of cattle.

I love all different kinds of sport and try to be outside whenever I can. My wife and I recently moved to Dublin from Glasgow, where we both studied at the University of Glasgow, and we are loving exploring Ireland. My wife, Joanna Crispell, works at the Science Gallery.
